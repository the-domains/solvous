You notice different things, hear them, become acutely aware of them (wind, road surface, bumps, traffic, the countryside). All of these things can become small pleasures or challenges depending on the situation.

Perhaps the hardest thing has been the wind to cope with. The Mistral is renowned and throughout the journey we witnessed the impact it can have on your journey time, energy and mood. The first two days we had 30/40kmh headwinds and crosswinds while the second two days were tailwinds and even no wind.

Day 1: St Pancras to Avignon. No Gear and No Idea.

This was where our adventure really began as by 5.30am on Monday morning everything went tits up as we became cyclists heading out on a cycling holiday with no cycles.
